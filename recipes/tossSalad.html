Toss Salad

Description

This recipe is for a healthy traditional toss salad that can be made with simple items from the store.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomotoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Bacon
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing

Steps

  1. Get a bowl, knife and cutting board
  2. cut the ingredients into small pieces
  3. put all ingredients in bowl
  4. Shake the bowl for 30 seconds
  5. Serve the salad in portions you can eat, Enjoy
