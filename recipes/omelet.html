Egg Meat Omelet

Description

This meal is a egg omelet stuffed with eggs, meat and cheese. All the best ingredients that a omelet can have in it.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • cheddar cheese
  • cooked pork or chicken chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic salt

Steps

  1. Heat pan and add olive oil
  2. Add chopped meat and eggs same time
  3. When eggs no longer liquid, add cheese
  4. Fold over the eggs
  5. Make sure cheese melts a bit then remove from pan
